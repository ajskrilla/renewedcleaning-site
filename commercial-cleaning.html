Commercial cleaning FAQs

What types of commercial spaces do you service?
Offices, retail and storefronts, common areas and hallways, gyms and community rooms, property-management units, and more. If you're not sure whether your space is a fit, just ask.
How can we be sure our property is being cleaned?
After every visit you receive a completed job report, and you can see scheduled, upcoming, and completed jobs any time in your client portal — full visibility, no guesswork.
Can aromatherapy products be used in common areas?
Yes. Our aromatherapy after-care is a great fit for lobbies, common areas, and shared spaces — in a scent you choose.
Do you provide after-hours cleaning?
Yes. We schedule around your business hours so cleaning never disrupts your team or your customers.
